About TotalHER

Built for HER. Backed by Women.

TotalHER - A New Kind of Social Space

TotalHER isn’t just another social app, it’s a revolution in how women connect, create, and express themselves online. We have built a bold, women-only platform where your voice is valued, your content shines, and your vibe is celebrated.

No judgment. No pressure. No distractions. Just a safe, empowering space to be your full, unapologetic self. From reels and stories to bookings and business tools, TotalHER gives you the power to scroll with purpose and post with pride.

Join the platform where women lead the feed!

Our Mission

To help every woman grow, express herself, and connect in a digital space made just for HER. We believe in safety, creativity, and the freedom to be yourself, because when women feel safe, they shine.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted, purpose-driven social platform for women. A space where fearless expression, real sisterhood, and meaningful opportunity come together, without noise, fear, or compromise.

How It All Started

For years, women have been showing up on social media, but not always feeling safe, heard, or respected. We were tired of being interrupted by unsolicited messages, overlooked by algorithms, and silenced in spaces that were never really designed with us in mind.

What if social media put women first?

Not as an afterthought. Not as a filtered version. But fully, proudly, and authentically.

That’s how TotalHER began, not as just another app, but as a response to what so many women have been waiting for: a platform where your voice leads the feed, a space where your content is celebrated, not scrutinized, a sisterhood where your safety isn’t optional but a priority.
