Welcome to TotalHER. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how TotalHER (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal information when you access or use the TotalHER website (collectively, the “Platform”).

By using TotalHER, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the Platform.

Who We Are

TotalHER is a global social networking platform exclusively for women, operated in accordance with the laws of the State of Georgia, United States. We are committed to creating a secure and empowering space where women can connect, express themselves, share content, collaborate, and grow personally and professionally.

Eligibility to Use TotalHER

TotalHER is available to individuals who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect or process personal data from anyone under the age of 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we may suspend or remove access to that account.

Information We Collect

When you register for or use TotalHER, we collect the following types of personal and usage information:

  • Identity Data: Name, email address, date of birth, and gender.
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address or entered information.
  • Profile Data: Photos, videos, bio, and other content you upload to your profile.
  • Verification Data: Details for account verification, stored securely and retained for the duration of account use.
  • Payment Information: Data provided during subscription purchases or other transactions.
  • Usage Data: Device identifiers, interactions, preferences, content engagement, and cookies.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as health records, government IDs, or biometric information.
