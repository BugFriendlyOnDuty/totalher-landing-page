TotalHER Cookie
Policy

Understanding Your Cookie Policy, Our Way.

Effective Date: 10/05/2025

Last Updated: 12/06/2025

At TotalHER, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users while delivering an empowering and seamless experience on our platform. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, the types of cookies we collect, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

For detailed information on how we protect and handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy.

By using TotalHER, you agree to our use of cookies as outlined in this policy. If you do not agree, you can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, etc.) when you visit websites. These cookies allow the website to recognize your device and store certain preferences or information to enhance your experience.

There are different types of cookies, including:

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies that remain on your device only for the duration of your visit. They are automatically deleted once you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a set period of time or until you manually delete them. They are used to remember your preferences or login information across sessions.
  • First-Party Cookies: These are cookies set directly by TotalHER.
  • Third-Party Cookies: These cookies are set by other parties, such as analytics providers, advertisers, or other social media platforms.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to provide, improve, and personalize our services, making your experience on TotalHER better. Here’s how we use cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functions of the website or mobile app, such as logging in, navigating between pages, and ensuring security.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect data on how visitors interact with our website or app, including the pages they visit and the links they click on. This helps us analyze traffic and improve the user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow TotalHER to remember choices you make (like your username or language preference) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver relevant ads to users. They track your browsing habits and ensure that the ads you see are tailored to your interests.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party cookies to provide certain services, including analytics, advertising, and social media integrations. These third-party service providers may set cookies to:

  • Analyze user behavior and improve our services
  • Serve relevant ads based on your browsing history
  • Enable social sharing features.

These third-party providers have their own privacy policies and are responsible for the use of cookies. You can manage or block these cookies through your browser settings.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit TotalHER, you will be prompted to consent to the use of cookies. You can accept or decline non-essential cookies. If you decline, you will still be able to access most of TotalHER’s features, but some functionalities may be limited.

You can also manage your cookie preferences at any time by adjusting your settings. If you want to block cookies altogether, you can do so by modifying your browser settings. However, please note that if you block all cookies, certain features of TotalHER may not function properly.

For instructions on how to manage cookies in different browsers, please visit the help section of your browser.

5. Cookie Retention

Cookies used by TotalHER may be stored on your device for varying durations, depending on their type. Session cookies are temporary and are deleted once you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device until manually deleted or for as long as necessary to fulfill their purpose.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the updated policy will be posted here with a new "Effective Date." We encourage you to periodically review this policy for any updates.

By continuing to use TotalHER after any updates to this policy, you accept the changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy or how we handle your data, please feel free to contact us at:

Thank you for trusting TotalHER. We are committed to making your experience safe, enjoyable, and empowering!
